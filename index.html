About Me



I’m a data analyst who specializes in data analysis, workflow automation, and web scraping. I work with real-world datasets that are often messy, incomplete, or inconsistent, and turn them into structured insights. My strength lies in data cleaning, transformation, trend and performance analysis, RFM segmentation, visual reporting, automating repetitive tasks and extracting data from websites using Python. I provide solutions that save time and improve decision-making.

My journey into data began with a strong love for mathematics, which led me to teach it as a school teacher — an experience that deepened my passion for logic and problem-solving. I later transitioned into web development, where I built websites for fun and this one also reflects my background in web development.

This portfolio highlights the kind of work I enjoy: practical, insight-driven, and built to solve real business problems through clean data and smart automation.

Customer Value Segmentation & Revenue Behaviour Analysis for E-Commerce Growth

Project Summary: Analyzed 42,992 e-commerce transactions using RFM segmentation to classify customers into top-value, potential, and low-engagement tiers, and to uncover revenue trends across age groups, countries, and product categories. This project delivers a comprehensive view of customer behaviour and revenue dynamics to support strategic growth initiatives. Seasonal demand shifted from mid-year to early-year, and a strong preference for cash-on-delivery highlighted trust and usability gaps in digital payments. The analysis was conducted using Python with pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, and xlsxwriter to ensure scalable, data-driven insights for decision-making.
